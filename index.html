BIO

Hi there, I'm Begoña Tremps, international journalist with more of two decades of experience. I've worked for news agencies, corporate press, national and international press. I started my career in Spain and have around 12 years of experience abroad: Ireland, Brazil, Portugal, Indonesia, Chile, France and Germany. I'm based in Berlin for five years now. I currently work as freelance for the biggest German News Agency (dpa) as well as for German and Spanish corporate press. I work comfortably in Spanish, English, German, French and have also labor experience in Portuguese. My main competencies are: communication, writing, translation, interpretation and photography.

MOTIVATIONS

Journalism is not a profession, it’s a passion. I intend to continue feeding this literary genre providing the perspective that my life path (professional and personal) has given me from Europe, Latin America, North America and Asia. As well as contributing with creativity towards digital and multimedia content to get the message across. Potential is huge. Let's explore it.
